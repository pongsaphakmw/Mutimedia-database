About the Paralympic Games

The Paralympic Games are a multi-sport event for athletes with disabilities, including physical and intellectual disabilities. These Games showcase the incredible abilities of athletes from all over the world, inspiring millions and promoting inclusivity and accessibility in sports. The Paris 2024 Paralympic Games promise to be a celebration of determination, skill, and sportsmanship.
